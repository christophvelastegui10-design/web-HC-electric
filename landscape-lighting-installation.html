How It Works

Our Seamless Process

From the initial layout to the final lighting reveal, we handle everything.

1

Design Consultation

We walk your property, identify key features to light, and plan fixture placement and wiring routes.

2

Free Quote

You receive a detailed quote covering fixtures, transformer, cabling, and labor. No surprises.

3

Installation Day

Fixtures are installed, low-voltage cable is buried, and everything is connected to the transformer.

4

Nighttime Demo

We return at dusk to adjust fixture angles, verify the look, and set your smart timer.
