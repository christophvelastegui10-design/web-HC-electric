Our Services

Residential Electrical

Complete electrical solutions designed for homeowners. Whether it's a single outlet or a full rewiring project, we handle it with care.

Commercial Electrical

Professional electrical services tailored for businesses, offices, retail spaces, and warehouses. Minimal downtime, maximum reliability.

  • Commercial wiring and fit-outs

  • Energy efficiency audits

Panel Upgrades & Repairs

Outdated panels are a safety hazard. We upgrade and repair electrical panels to meet modern demands safely and efficiently.

  • Breaker replacement and repair
  • Load balancing and capacity increases
  • Sub-panel installation
  • Electrical inspections

Emergency Repairs

When the power goes out or something fails, every minute counts. Our emergency team responds fast, any hour of the day or night.

  • Short circuit and fault resolution
  • Sparking wire and outlet repair
  • Storm damage restoration

  • Smart charger compatibility

Lighting Design & Installation
